Sam's Chicken Stew Recipe


Ingredients


  • Chop one onion
  • 1 teaspoon of masala
  • Teaspoon of garlic and ginger paste
  • Two spoon of olive oil 5 minutes*
  • Eight pieces of chicken thighs

Method


  1. Pour your onion,masala and olive oil in a pot.
  2. Put the pot on a stove opened on number 4 hot and stir until starting to be brown.
  3. Pour your ginger and garlic paste and stir less that a minute.
  4. Put your chicken pieces and close, let it heat for 10 minutes.
